# 🚀 Last Minute Life Saver

> **"Most productivity apps remind you about deadlines. We actively prevent you from missing them."**

An AI-powered **Deadline Rescue System** that predicts missed deadlines, creates intelligent execution plans, intervenes against procrastination, and actively helps users complete their work before it's too late.

Built for the **Google AI Hackathon**.

---

# 🌟 The Problem

Students, professionals, and entrepreneurs don't usually miss deadlines because they forget.

They miss them because they:

* Keep postponing important work
* Underestimate the effort required
* Don't know where to start
* Become overwhelmed
* Enter panic mode too late

Traditional productivity apps send reminders.

**Last Minute Life Saver goes one step further—it becomes your AI rescue partner.**

---

# 💡 Our Solution

Instead of asking,

> *"What should I remember?"*

our AI continuously answers,

> **"What should I do RIGHT NOW to avoid future failure?"**

The application predicts risks, creates rescue plans, and guides users until the mission is complete.

# ✨ Key Features

## 🎯 AI Task Breakdown

Large goals are automatically converted into actionable subtasks.

Example:

```

Research

↓

Planning

↓

Frontend

↓

Backend

↓

Testing

↓

Deployment
```

---

## 📈 Deadline Survival Score

Every task receives an AI-generated probability of successful completion.

Example:

```
Google STEP Application

Survival Score

28%

⚠ High Risk
```

Instead of simply showing deadlines, the system predicts which tasks are actually in danger.

---

## 🧠 Future Self Simulator

Visualizes two possible futures.

### Start Today

* Low Stress
* High Completion Probability
* Better Work-Life Balance

### Delay

* High Stress
* Low Completion Probability
* Last-Minute Panic

Helping users understand the real cost of procrastination.

## 🗓 Reverse Calendar Planning

Rather than displaying a standard calendar, Gemini works backwards from the deadline to automatically create a personalized execution plan.

```
Deadline

↓

Testing

↓

Development

↓

Planning

↓

Research

↓

Today
```

The plan can be synced directly to Google Calendar.

---

## 🚨 Emergency Rescue Mode

When deadlines are dangerously close, the application automatically switches into Rescue Mode.

It generates:

* Hour-by-hour execution plans
* Focus sessions
* Task prioritization
* Submission checklists

---

## 🧬 Personalized Productivity

The AI continuously learns:

* Productive hours
* Focus duration
* Completion patterns
* Habit consistency

to generate increasingly personalized recommendations.

---

## 📊 Habit Intelligence

Tracks habits and correlates them with productivity.

Example:

> "You complete coding tasks 2× faster after maintaining a 5-day study streak."
